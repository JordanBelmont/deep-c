Project Blue Beam

Summary

Project Blue Beam is the name of a theoretical operation planned by NASA and orchestrated by global elites to stage a supernatural or extraterrestrial event with the goal of establishing a totalitarian world government.

The theory purports that satellites, holograms, drones and frequency-based technology will be used to simulate an alien invasion or a biblical event, causing widescale panic and ushering in a government to bring about order.

While it currently stands as just a theory, there are those that believe it's only a matter of time before it happens, while others believe it has already begun based on world events.

History

In 1952, a document from the Assistant Director of the Office of Scientific Intelligence H. Marshall Chadwell, entitled "Unidentified Flying Objects (Flying Saucers)" was sent to the Director of the CIA. In the document, Chadwell outlines the psychological apsects of world-wide UFO sightings and his recommendations:


"With a State-controlled press, this could result only from an official policy decision. The question, therefore, arises to whether or not these sightings:

(1) could be controlled,

(2) could be predicted, and

(3) could be used from a psychological warfare point of view, either offensively or defensively."

- H. Marshall Chadwell,
"Unidentified Flying Objects (Flying Saucers)"
1972


In 1975, Screenwriter Gene Roddenberry wrote a screenplay for his TV show Star Trek. The plot of the screenplay saw aliens sending religious figures to Earth via holograms in order to spread their own religion to humanity and ultimately, conquer the planet. Roddenberry's screenplay was rejected, but he published the story as a novel instead, entitled The God-Thing.

On September 21, 1987, President Ronald Reagan addressed the United Nations in New York:


"I occasionally think how quickly our differences worldwide would vanish if we were facing an alien threat from outside this world."

- Ronald Reagan,
1987


In 1989, Physicist Robert Lazar blew the whistle on his time working for the U.S. government, reverse engineering an extraterrestrial spacecraft. According to Lazar, he was employed at "S-4", a hidden black-site facility near Area 51 that housed nine extraterrestrial flying saucers. Lazar has gone on to be derided by ufologists for providing no real evidence to his claims.

In 1994, writer and journalist Serge Monast published his book Project Blue Beam, which detailed how the United Nations were attempting to use futuristic technology through NASA to start a New World Order under a unified religion led by the Antichrist.

In September 1996, Monast's children, who were being homeschooled by Monast, were taken from him and made wards of the state to give them a public education.

On December 4, Monast was arrested for spreading misinformation ,spending the night in jail. The following day, he died of a heart attack at the age of 51. Some theorise that his death was brought on by poisoning.

A January 2000 issue of Jane's International Defense Review featured an article about Special Access Programs, projects with a high enough security clearance that those involved were authorised to lie publicly to safeguard their existence.

In 2001, American Ufologist Steven Greer released his book Disclosure: Military and Government Witnesses Reveal the Greatest Secrets in Modern History, in it, Dr. Carol Rosin, assistant to German aerospace engineer and Operation Paperclip asset Werner von Braun, explained how von Braun revealed to her the U.S. government's step-by-step plan for a weaponization of space before he died:


"The strategy that Werner Von Braun taught me was that first the Russians are going to be considered to be the enemy... Then terrorists would be identified. We heard a lot about terrorism. Then we were going to identify third-world country 'crazies.'... The next enemy was asteroids... the last card is the alien card. We are going to have to build space-based weapons against aliens and all of it is a lie."

- Steven M Greer,
Disclosure: Military and Government Witnesses Reveal the Greatest Secrets in Modern History
Crossing Point, 2001


On November 14, 2004, Commander David Fravor tracked an unusual aircraft off the coast of California. The aircraft appeared at 80,000 ft before descending rapidly and stopping at 20,000 ft where it hovered until it ascended completely out of sight. Fravor went on to describe the aircraft as a "tic tac" based on its shape in infrared imaging.

In June 2012, France held the first drone display at the Meet Your Creator, Saatchi & Saatchi New Directors' Showcase in Cannes, where several drones mounted with mirrors used LEDs to create a 3D display in the sky.

In January 2015, two separate incidents occured in which U.S. fighter pilots recorded unidentified aerial objects. The videos would later go on to be dubbed the "GIMBAL" and "GOFAST" videos.

Later that year, a city was witnessed floating in the sky above Chinese cities Foshan and Jiangxi. Scientists claimed it was a rare phenomenon known as fata morgana: a mirage due to light bending through layers of air with different temperatures.

In 2017, Korean researchers at KAIST developed a method that would improve holographic technology using wavefront-shaping to control scattered light, aiding in the process of displaying dynamic 3D holograms without the need of special glasses.

On June 25, 2021, a report was released by the Office of the Director of National Intelligence that indicated that many UAP sightings were physical objects, that they could prove to be a security risk, and that in most instances they were unexplainable.

On January 28, 2024, Noland Arbaugh was the first recipient to receive a Neuralink: a chip implant entitled "Telepathy" that allows for brain-computer interface.

In February, Tokyo began displaying the world's largest permanents architectural projection-mapped display at their "Tokyo Night & Light" show. Using Panasonic projectors, the display sees the Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building transformed into a dynamic canvas of music and lights.

Around October, an anonymous whistleblower released a classified document entitled "IMMACULATE CONSTELLATION" that detailed a Special Access Program that used military intelligence resources to collect imagery on UAPs.

In December, Dubai began hosting regular drone shows for their Dubai Shopping Festival.

In 2025, Croatian Singer Marko Perković Thompson performed at the Zagreb Hippodrome. His concert featured 1,000 coordinated drones displaying religious iconography and notable quotations.

Phases

According to Monast's research, Project Blue Beam would be enacted through a combination of advanced technology and psychological warfare to create illusions that are indistinguishable from reality.

The project would take place over four phases, each


Phase 1: Apostasy

The first step of Blue Beam would see the deconstruction of all major world religions. Artificially induced earthquakes in strategic spots across the planet would uncover new archeological discoveries that will cast doubt on the beliefs of every religion.

With religious doctrine in question, science would be able to discredit and supplant faith. Those in crisis of faith would be more likely to accept a new religion.


Phase 2: Illusions

Using a combination of drone and satellite technology, 3D images and sounds would be projected into the sky all across the planet. Holograms of significant religious figures would merge to create a new idol: the Antichrist.

Speaking to people in their own, native language, this new deity would convince humanity that the old religions have brought only devastation, and they must be abolished to make way for a new religion that must unify everyone against an impending spiritual or supernatural event.


Phase 3: Telepathy

Through the manipulation of electronically augmented two-way communication, people will receive what they believe to be telepathic messaging from an Antichrist.

With the amount of personal data stored on computers, the messaging can be directed at each individual on a personal level and can take the form of whatever would be most convincing to that individual.


Phase 4: Cataclysm

The final phase would see the creation of a manufactured crisis, such as a simulated extraterrestrial invasion, prompting major nations to respond by using nuclear weapons. The fallout would cause mass panic and lead to a complete collapse of society.

With the world plunged into chaos, humanity would demand safety, even at the cost of personal freedoms. An outraged populous would demand governments immediately disarm, leaving the New World Order in charge as the only, uncontested super power.

Attestation

If Project Blue Beam were to exist, it would be difficult to outright prove the existence of such a covert operation, but looking through the historical timeline and deconstructing the stages of how the project would be enacted can shed some light on its likelihood.

Much speculation surrounds the untimely death of Serge Monast, the progenitor of the Blue Beam theory. Monast suffered a heart attack at the age of 51, one day after being arrested for misinformation, despite being considered healthy. The timing of his death, only two years after publishing Project Blue Beam and one year after publishing Les Protocoles de Toronto, both of which exposed the global domination plans of powerful elites, is considered by many to be more than mere coincidence. If the cause of Monast's death was assassination, it wouldn't be the first time a whistleblower would have conveniently died under strange circumstances. In 2024, John Barnett, the whistleblower on Boeing Commercial Airplanes' malpractice, was found dead of a gunshot wound after failing to report for his deposition. Similarly, Suchir Balaji who was a former OpenAI engineer, was found dead from a gunshot wound after making claims that OpenAI violated AI copyright laws.

When it comes to the first phase of Blue Beam, the ability to manufacture earthquakes is an actual possibility. It's believed that fracking, mining, wastewater disposal, carbon storage or underground nuclear testing all put immense stress on the Earth's crust and can induce seismicity. In 2008, an earthquake in Sichuan, China is thought to have been caused by the nearby Zipingpu Dam due to the weight of the reservoir's water and proximity to a fault line, while wastewater injection wells were found to be the epicenter of the 2011 Oklahoma earthquake.

In 1947, the Dead Sea Scrolls were found in clay pots found in a cave near Qumran. The scrolls include some of the earliest texts from the Hebrew Bible, fundamentally changing what was known at the time of the origins of Judaism. It's not far-fetched to believe that modern discoveries of ancient relics couldn't have a similar impact on the way humanity interprets religious doctrine.
